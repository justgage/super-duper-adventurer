Recent college graduate Ryden Malby has a grand plan: Find a fabulous apartment and land a job at a prestigious publishing house. However, her rival Jessica steals her dream job, and Ryden has to move back in with her weird family while she sorts out her life. A stack of rejection letters grows while she hangs out with her best friend and her neighbor, but if she is to gain traction in her life again, Ryden will have to make a new plan.
